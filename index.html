RideKeep Support

RideKeep is a private, offline maintenance and expense log for motorcycle riders. Track chain lubes, oil changes, tyres, inspections and more — by mileage or by date — and get reminded before anything is due.

Need help?

For questions, bug reports, or feature requests, email us at gulerrismet@gmail.com. We usually reply within a couple of days.

Frequently asked

Is my data private? Yes. Everything you enter stays on your device. RideKeep has no account and no cloud server.

How do reminders work? Reminders trigger by distance (km) or by date — whichever comes first. Mark a job done and the next interval is scheduled automatically.

How do I manage my subscription? Open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your name, then Subscriptions, and select RideKeep.
